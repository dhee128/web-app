Skillet Baked Almond-and-Banana Oatmeal

This Skillet Baked Almond-and-Banana Oatmeal is a nutritious and delicious breakfast option that combines the creamy texture of baked oats with the natural sweetness of bananas and the crunchy goodness of almonds. It’s perfect for a comforting start to your day!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup sliced almonds
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup or honey
  • 1 1/2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt or non-dairy yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Optional: fresh fruit or additional nuts for topping

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the rolled oats, sliced almonds, ground cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the milk, Greek yogurt, maple syrup (or honey), and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients and mix well.
  5. Fold in the sliced bananas.
  6. Pour the oatmeal mixture into a 10-inch skillet or an oven-safe baking dish.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-35 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the oats are cooked through.
  8. Allow the baked oatmeal to cool slightly before serving. Top with fresh fruit or additional nuts if desired.
