Bowl of Oatmeal Porridge with Banana

This comforting bowl of oatmeal porridge with banana is a wholesome and satisfying breakfast. Creamy oats topped with fresh banana slices create a nutritious start to your day, and you can customize it with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 2 cups water or non-dairy milk (such as almond, soy, or oat milk)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 ripe banana, sliced
  • Additional toppings (optional): nuts, seeds, dried fruit, fresh berries

Method

  1. In a medium saucepan, bring the water or non-dairy milk to a boil.
  2. Add the rolled oats and ground cinnamon to the boiling liquid. Reduce the heat to low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for about 5-7 minutes or until the oats are tender and the mixture has thickened.
  3. If desired, stir in the maple syrup or honey to sweeten the oatmeal.
  4. Pour the cooked oatmeal into a bowl.
  5. Top with sliced banana and any additional toppings of your choice, such as nuts, seeds, dried fruit, or fresh berries.
