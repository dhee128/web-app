Banana Smoothie

This Banana Smoothie is a quick, delicious, and nutritious drink perfect for breakfast or a snack. It's creamy, naturally sweet, and packed with flavor, making it a great way to start your day or refuel after a workout.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 1 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond, soy, or oat milk)
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt or plant-based yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for extra sweetness)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • 1/2 cup ice cubes (optional, for a chilled smoothie)
  • Additional toppings (optional): sliced banana, granola, chia seeds

Method

  1. In a blender, combine the ripe bananas, non-dairy milk, Greek yogurt, honey or maple syrup, and vanilla extract if using.
  2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a colder smoothie, add ice cubes and blend again until well combined.
  3. Pour the smoothie into a glass.
  4. Top with additional slices of banana, granola, or chia seeds if desired.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
