Apple Cake

This apple cake is moist and flavorful, made with fresh apples and a hint of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 3 large apples, peeled and diced
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 eggs

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and cinnamon.
  3. In another bowl, beat the butter and sugar until fluffy. Add the eggs one at a time.
  4. Stir in the diced apples.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients and mix until combined.
  6. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan and bake for 45 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
