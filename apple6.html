Southern Fried Apples

Southern Fried Apples are a delicious and comforting side dish. Perfect for a cozy breakfast or as a sweet addition to any meal, these apples are cooked until tender and caramelized with a hint of cinnamon.

Ingredients

  • 4 medium apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup chopped pecans (optional)

Method

  1. In a large skillet, melt the butter over medium heat.
  2. Add the sliced apples to the skillet and cook, stirring occasionally, until they start to soften, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Stir in the brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Continue cooking for another 5 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the sauce has thickened slightly.
  4. Add the water to the skillet and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to help dissolve the sugar and create a syrup.
  5. If using, stir in the chopped pecans and cook for another minute.
  6. Serve warm, either on its own or as a topping for pancakes, waffles, or ice cream.
