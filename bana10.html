Rich Butter Banana Cake

This Rich Butter Banana Cake is a decadent treat with a buttery, moist crumb and a delightful banana flavor. Perfect for special occasions or a sweet indulgence, this cake combines the richness of butter with the natural sweetness of ripe bananas.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup sour cream or plain yogurt
  • 1 cup mashed ripe bananas (about 2-3 bananas)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Powdered sugar for dusting (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a 9-inch round cake pan or line it with parchment paper.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the softened butter and granulated sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add the eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition. Mix in the vanilla extract.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, alternating with the sour cream or yogurt, mixing until just combined.
  6. Fold in the mashed bananas and, if using, the ground cinnamon.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan and spread it evenly.
  8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  9. Allow the cake to cool in the pan for 10 minutes before transferring it to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Once the cake is completely cool, dust with powdered sugar if desired. Slice and serve.
