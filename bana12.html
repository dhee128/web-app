Banana Puff Stuffed in Banana Leaf

Banana Puff Stuffed in Banana Leaf is a traditional and flavorful dish where ripe bananas are blended with spices and stuffed in banana leaves before being steamed or grilled. This dish combines the natural sweetness of bananas with aromatic spices for a delightful treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas, peeled and mashed
  • 1/2 cup grated coconut (fresh or desiccated)
  • 1/4 cup jaggery or brown sugar, grated
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts (optional, such as cashews or almonds)
  • Banana leaves, cut into squares (for wrapping)
  • Water (for steaming)

Method

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed bananas, grated coconut, jaggery (or brown sugar), cardamom powder, cinnamon powder, and chopped nuts if using. Mix well until all ingredients are evenly combined.
  2. Soften the banana leaves by passing them over an open flame or blanching them in boiling water for a few seconds. This makes them pliable and easier to fold.
  3. Cut the banana leaves into squares, approximately 6-8 inches wide.
  4. Place a spoonful of the banana mixture in the center of each banana leaf square.
  5. Fold the edges of the banana leaf over the filling to form a neat package. Secure with toothpicks if necessary.
  6. Arrange the wrapped puffs in a steamer basket or on a rack over a pot of boiling water. Steam for 15-20 minutes, or until the puffs are firm and cooked through.
  7. Allow the puffs to cool slightly before unwrapping. Serve warm as a delicious snack or dessert.
