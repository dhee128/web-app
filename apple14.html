Breakfast Apple Crisp

This Breakfast Apple Crisp is a healthy and delicious way to start your day. With tender apples and a crunchy, oat-based topping, it’s perfect for breakfast or a wholesome snack.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups peeled, cored, and sliced apples (about 4 medium apples)
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small pieces
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional, such as walnuts or pecans)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease a 9x9-inch baking dish or similar-sized dish.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the sliced apples with granulated sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss until the apples are well coated.
  3. Transfer the apple mixture to the prepared baking dish and spread it out evenly.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, and brown sugar. Add the cold butter pieces and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  5. If using, stir in the chopped nuts.
  6. Sprinkle the crisp topping evenly over the apple mixture in the baking dish.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until the topping is golden brown and the apples are tender.
  8. Allow to cool slightly before serving. Enjoy warm, optionally with a dollop of Greek yogurt or a splash of milk.
