Simple Vegan Apple Pie with Ice Cream

This Simple Vegan Apple Pie combines a deliciously spiced apple filling with a flaky, buttery vegan crust. Served with a scoop of dairy-free ice cream, it’s a delightful dessert that’s both comforting and cruelty-free.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup vegan butter, cold and cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup ice water (more if needed)
  • 4 cups apples, peeled, cored, and sliced
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • Dairy-free ice cream (store-bought or homemade)

Method

  1. In a large bowl, combine flour and sugar. Add cold vegan butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work it into the flour until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs.
  2. Gradually add ice water, 1 tablespoon at a time, mixing until the dough just comes together. Divide the dough into two discs, wrap in plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  3. In a large bowl, combine sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and lemon juice. Toss to coat the apples evenly.
  4. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  5. On a floured surface, roll out one disc of dough to fit a 9-inch pie plate. Place the rolled-out dough into the pie plate, trimming any excess.
  6. Fill with the apple mixture and dot with a few small pieces of vegan butter if desired.
  7. Roll out the second disc of dough and place over the apple filling. Trim and crimp the edges to seal. Cut a few slits in the top crust to allow steam to escape.
  8. Brush the top crust with a little water and sprinkle with sugar if desired.
  9. Bake for 50-60 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbling.
  10. Let the pie cool before serving. Serve with a scoop of dairy-free ice cream for an extra treat.
