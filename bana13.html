Banana Ice Cream

This Banana Ice Cream is a simple and delicious homemade treat that captures the natural sweetness of ripe bananas. With no need for an ice cream maker, it's perfect for a quick and easy dessert that's both creamy and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream or full-fat coconut milk (for a dairy-free option)
  • 1/4 cup honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Optional: 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon or chocolate chips for mix-ins

Method

  1. Peel the ripe bananas and cut them into small slices. Arrange the slices in a single layer on a baking sheet or plate lined with parchment paper.
  2. Freeze the banana slices for at least 2 hours, or until they are completely frozen.
  3. Place the frozen banana slices in a food processor or blender.
  4. Add the heavy cream (or coconut milk), honey (or maple syrup), and vanilla extract.
  5. Blend until the mixture is smooth and creamy. You may need to stop and scrape down the sides of the bowl occasionally.
  6. If desired, add ground cinnamon or chocolate chips and pulse briefly to incorporate.
  7. Serve the banana ice cream immediately for a soft-serve texture or transfer it to a lidded container and freeze for 1-2 hours for a firmer consistency.
