Salted Caramel Banana Bread Puddings

Salted Caramel Banana Bread Puddings are a decadent and comforting dessert combining rich caramel sauce with tender banana bread pudding. Perfect for a special treat or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed day-old banana bread
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt (or to taste)

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease 6 ramekins or a similar-sized baking dish.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together the milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Add the cubed banana bread to the mixture and gently fold until the bread is well coated and starts to soak up the liquid.
  4. Divide the mixture evenly among the prepared ramekins or baking dish.
  5. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the pudding is set and the tops are golden brown. Allow to cool slightly before serving.
  6. In a medium saucepan over medium heat, melt the granulated sugar, stirring constantly until it turns into a golden caramel color.
  7. Once melted, carefully add the butter and continue to stir until fully combined and smooth.
  8. Slowly pour in the heavy cream while stirring, and cook until the sauce is smooth and thickened. Remove from heat and stir in the sea salt.
  9. Allow the caramel sauce to cool slightly before drizzling over the warm banana bread puddings.
