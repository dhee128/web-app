Apple Balsamic Flatbread

This Apple Balsamic Flatbread combines the sweet and tangy flavors of apple and balsamic vinegar with a crispy flatbread base. It's a perfect appetizer or light meal that's both flavorful and elegant.

Ingredients

  • 1 store-bought flatbread or pizza dough
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 large apple, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled goat cheese
  • 1/4 cup thinly sliced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped walnuts
  • 1/4 cup balsamic glaze
  • Fresh thyme leaves for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Place the flatbread or pizza dough on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  2. Brush the flatbread with olive oil and bake for 5-7 minutes, or until it starts to crisp up.
  3. Remove from the oven and arrange the apple slices evenly over the partially baked flatbread.
  4. Top with crumbled goat cheese, sliced red onion, and chopped walnuts.
  5. Return to the oven and bake for an additional 8-10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and the flatbread is crispy.
  6. Drizzle with balsamic glaze before serving. Garnish with fresh thyme leaves if desired.
  7. Slice and serve warm.
