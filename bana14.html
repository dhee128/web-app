Instant Raw Banana Dosa

Instant Raw Banana Dosa is a quick and nutritious dosa made using raw bananas. This easy-to-make recipe is perfect for a healthy breakfast or snack. The dosa has a unique flavor and is packed with the benefits of raw bananas.

Ingredients

  • 2 raw bananas (green or semi-ripe)
  • 1/2 cup rice flour
  • 1/4 cup grated coconut
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1-2 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for cooking

Method

  1. Peel and cut the raw bananas into chunks. Steam them until they are tender. You can also boil them if you prefer.
  2. Once cooked, mash the bananas in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add rice flour, grated coconut, chopped onions, green chilies, cumin seeds, turmeric powder, and salt to the mashed bananas. Mix well to form a smooth batter. If the batter is too thick, you can add a little water to achieve the desired consistency.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease it with oil.
  5. Pour a ladleful of the batter onto the skillet and spread it into a thin, even layer using the back of the ladle.
  6. Cook for 2-3 minutes or until the edges start to lift and the bottom is golden brown. Flip and cook for another 1-2 minutes on the other side.
  7. Remove the dosa from the skillet and repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve the Instant Raw Banana Dosa hot with your favorite chutney or sambar.
