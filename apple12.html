Parm-Crusted Grilled Cheese

This Parm-Crusted Grilled Cheese with Apple adds a sweet and crunchy twist to the classic grilled cheese sandwich. The Parmesan crust provides a crispy texture, while the apple slices add a delightful hint of sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (your choice, such as sourdough or whole wheat)
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 slices of cheese (such as cheddar, Swiss, or American)
  • 1 apple, cored and thinly sliced (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp)
  • Optional: a drizzle of honey or a sprinkle of cinnamon

Method

  1. Preheat a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Spread a thin layer of butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. On a plate, spread out the grated Parmesan cheese. Press the buttered side of each slice of bread into the Parmesan cheese to coat it evenly.
  4. Place one slice of bread, Parmesan-coated side down, in the preheated skillet.
  5. Layer a slice of cheese on top of the bread, then add a few apple slices and any optional toppings (honey or cinnamon) if using.
  6. Top with the second slice of bread, buttered side up, and press down gently.
  7. Cook for 3-4 minutes on each side, or until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted. Adjust the heat as needed to prevent burning.
  8. Remove from the skillet and let the sandwich cool for a minute before slicing in half. Serve warm.
