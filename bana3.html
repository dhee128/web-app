Banana-Stuffed Aquafaba French Toast

Banana-Stuffed Aquafaba French Toast is a delicious and vegan-friendly twist on a classic breakfast favorite. With creamy banana filling and a light, crispy exterior, this dish is perfect for a special brunch or a cozy breakfast at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup aquafaba (chickpea brine)
  • 1/4 cup non-dairy milk (such as almond, soy, or oat milk)
  • 1 tablespoon ground flaxseed
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 6 slices of bread (use your favorite type, preferably stale or slightly dry)
  • Vegan butter or oil for frying
  • Additional maple syrup for serving
  • Fresh fruit or powdered sugar for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. In a bowl, mix together the mashed bananas, maple syrup, and ground cinnamon until well combined. Set aside.
  2. In a shallow dish, whisk together the aquafaba, non-dairy milk, ground flaxseed, maple syrup, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  3. Spread a generous amount of the banana mixture on one slice of bread, then top with another slice to make a sandwich. Repeat with the remaining bread and filling.
  4. Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat and lightly grease with vegan butter or oil.
  5. Dip each sandwich in the French toast batter, coating both sides well.
  6. Cook the sandwiches in the skillet for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown and crispy. Add more butter or oil to the pan as needed.
  7. Serve the banana-stuffed French toast warm, drizzled with maple syrup and garnished with fresh fruit or powdered sugar if desired.
