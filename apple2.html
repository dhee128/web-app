Baked Apples

These baked apples are a warm and comforting treat, perfect for a cozy evening. Stuffed with a cinnamon-sugar mixture and baked until tender.

Ingredients

  • 4 large apples (such as Honeycrisp or Fuji)
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1/4 cup raisins
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup water

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Core the apples, leaving the bottoms intact to hold the filling.
  3. In a small bowl, mix the brown sugar, nuts, raisins, and cinnamon.
  4. Stuff each apple with the sugar mixture and place them in a baking dish.
  5. Place a small piece of butter on top of each apple.
  6. Pour water into the bottom of the baking dish.
  7. Bake for 25-30 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the filling is bubbly.
