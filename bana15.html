Banana Bread Overnight Oats

Banana Bread Overnight Oats combine the comforting flavors of banana bread with the convenience of overnight oats. This healthy breakfast option is perfect for busy mornings, offering a sweet and satisfying start to your day with minimal prep time.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/4 cup plain Greek yogurt or non-dairy yogurt
  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder (optional, for a fluffier texture)
  • 1 tablespoon chopped nuts or chocolate chips (optional, for topping)

Method

  1. In a mixing bowl or a mason jar, combine the rolled oats, milk, Greek yogurt, mashed banana, honey (or maple syrup), vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and baking powder if using.
  2. Mix well until all ingredients are thoroughly combined.
  3. Cover the bowl or jar and refrigerate overnight, or for at least 6 hours. This allows the oats to absorb the liquid and flavors, resulting in a creamy texture.
  4. The next morning, give the oats a good stir. Top with chopped nuts, chocolate chips, or additional banana slices if desired.
  5. Enjoy your Banana Bread Overnight Oats cold, straight from the refrigerator!
