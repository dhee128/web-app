Cardamom and Cinnamon Poached Apples

These poached apples are infused with aromatic cardamom and cinnamon, creating a delicately spiced and fragrant dessert. Perfect for a light, elegant treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 large apples (such as Gala or Braeburn)
  • 2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 cinnamon sticks
  • 5-6 cardamom pods, slightly crushed
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Method

  1. Peel and core the apples, leaving them whole.
  2. In a large saucepan, combine the water, sugar, cinnamon sticks, cardamom pods, and vanilla extract.
  3. Bring the mixture to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally until the sugar has dissolved.
  4. Add the apples to the saucepan and ensure they are submerged in the liquid.
  5. Cover and simmer for about 20-25 minutes, or until the apples are tender but still hold their shape.
  6. Remove the apples from the saucepan and set aside.
  7. Increase the heat and reduce the poaching liquid to a syrupy consistency.
  8. Stir in the lemon juice and pour the syrup over the poached apples.
  9. Let cool before serving, or refrigerate for later use.
