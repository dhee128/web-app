Banana Toast with Blueberries and Strawberries

Banana Toast with Blueberries and Strawberries is a simple and delicious breakfast or snack option. The sweetness of ripe bananas, paired with fresh blueberries and strawberries on toasted bread, makes for a delightful and nutritious treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of whole grain or your preferred bread
  • 2 ripe bananas, sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh blueberries
  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 2 tablespoons almond butter or peanut butter (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional)
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Method

  1. Toast the slices of bread to your desired level of crispiness using a toaster or toaster oven.
  2. If using, spread a layer of almond butter or peanut butter on each slice of toast.
  3. Arrange the banana slices evenly on top of the toast.
  4. Top with fresh blueberries and strawberries.
  5. Drizzle with honey or maple syrup if desired.
  6. Sprinkle with ground cinnamon for extra flavor.
  7. Garnish with mint leaves if using.
