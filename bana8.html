Banana Cream Pie

Banana Cream Pie is a classic dessert featuring a creamy banana filling nestled in a buttery pie crust and topped with whipped cream. It’s a delightful treat that's perfect for any occasion, bringing together the flavors of ripe bananas and rich vanilla cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 3 large ripe bananas
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 3 large egg yolks
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a medium bowl, combine graham cracker crumbs, granulated sugar, and melted butter. Mix until well combined.
  3. Press the mixture into the bottom and up the sides of a 9-inch pie pan. Bake for 8-10 minutes or until lightly golden. Allow to cool completely.
  4. Slice the bananas and arrange them in an even layer over the cooled pie crust.
  5. In a medium saucepan, whisk together granulated sugar, flour, and salt. Gradually whisk in the milk.
  6. Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture comes to a boil and thickens.
  7. In a small bowl, lightly beat the egg yolks. Gradually add a small amount of the hot mixture to the yolks, then whisk the yolk mixture back into the saucepan.
  8. Continue cooking for an additional 2-3 minutes, then remove from heat and stir in the butter and vanilla extract.
  9. Pour the filling over the bananas in the pie crust. Smooth the top with a spatula. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or until set.
  10. In a medium bowl, beat the heavy cream, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  11. Spread the whipped cream over the chilled pie.
  12. Slice and serve the pie. Enjoy!
